Beneath the Crust: Deep Dive into Volcanic Data




This dynamic dashboard unveils the fascinating realm of global volcanic activity, blending historical eruption timelines with geographical mappings and demographic insights. Through interactive visualizations, it portrays the distribution, types, and impact of volcanoes on human settlements across various elevations. Each chart invites exploration into the intricate dance between Earth's fiery nature and human habitation, offering a comprehensive lens on the delicate balance of living with these majestic yet formidable natural wonders.
